Our Story

Crafted with Passion,
Served with Love

At Dako Cafe, we believe every cup tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Dukem, we've been serving the community with the finest Ethiopian coffee, freshly squeezed juices, and delicious food since day one.

Our beans are carefully sourced from the best Ethiopian coffee farms, roasted to perfection, and brewed with expertise. From the rich aroma of our macchiato to the vibrant freshness of our mango juice — every sip is an experience.
